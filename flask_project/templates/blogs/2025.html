Technology Crisis • 2025

The Pressure of Automation & AI

In 2025, software companies entered a new era where Artificial Intelligence and automation tools rapidly transformed the industry. Businesses focused on speed, productivity, and cost reduction, creating both opportunities and uncertainty for developers worldwide.

Future of Software Companies

AI Started Replacing Traditional Workflows

Automation tools became powerful enough to handle repetitive software tasks such as testing, debugging, documentation, customer support, and basic code generation.

Companies reduced operational costs by integrating AI systems into daily workflows. Developers were expected to work faster, smarter, and adapt continuously to new technologies.

While AI improved productivity, many professionals feared job instability as businesses started prioritizing automation over traditional hiring methods.
